📜 HinokamiMC Server Rules

Maintaining a respectful and enjoyable environment for all players.

⚖️ General Conduct

  • Be respectful to all players and staff members.
  • No harassment, discrimination, or hate speech.
  • Avoid spamming, advertising, or disruptive behavior.
  • Use appropriate language; keep chat clean and friendly.

🛡️ Gameplay Rules

  • No cheating, hacking, or use of unauthorized mods.
  • Griefing and stealing are prohibited.
  • Respect land claims and property boundaries.
  • Do not exploit bugs or glitches; report them to staff.

👥 Community Guidelines

  • Follow the directions of staff members at all times.
  • Do not impersonate staff or other players.
  • Keep discussions appropriate for all age groups.
  • Report any rule violations to staff promptly.
