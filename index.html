🌟 What is HinokamiMC?

HinokamiMC is not your average copy-paste Minecraft server. Inspired by the world of Demon Slayer, it delivers a uniquely immersive survival multiplayer experience packed with lore, progression, and community-driven gameplay.

Join the ranks of demon slayers or embrace the darkness as a demon. With custom Breathing Styles, demon powers, tournaments, cosmetics, and land claiming—this isn’t just another SMP. It’s a world where your choices shape your destiny.
