🌟 About HinokamiMC

HinokamiMC is a custom survival multiplayer (SMP) Minecraft server inspired by the world of Demon Slayer. Master your breathing style, battle demons and slayers, and carve your legacy in an ever-evolving world filled with lore, PvP, and exploration.

🔥 Why We're Different

Tired of lifeless copy-paste servers? So are we.

  • 🎮 Fully Custom Progression: Level up your breathing techniques or demon abilities with unique mechanics designed exclusively for HinokamiMC.
  • 📜 Original Lore and Events: Explore seasonal storylines, immersive quests, and a world with rich history that evolves with the community.
  • ⚙️ Tailor-Made Plugins: We don’t rely on off-the-shelf setups. Our features are handcrafted by our development team to provide a truly original SMP experience.
  • 🎨 Dedicated Creative Team: From stunning builds to finely-tuned aesthetics, everything you see is custom-built for immersion and uniqueness.

⚔️ Core Features

  • 🗡️ Breathing Styles & Forms: Unlock and evolve your combat technique tree.
  • 👹 Demon Progression: Harness dark powers and climb the ranks.
  • 🏰 Land Claiming: Defend your base and allies with the Lands plugin.
  • 🏆 PvP Tournaments: Compete monthly to become the top warrior.
  • 💎 Cosmetics & Enchants: Show off your style with custom visual effects.
